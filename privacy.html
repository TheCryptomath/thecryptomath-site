Privacy Policy

Last updated February 4, 2026

What this site does

thecryptomath.com is a static website. It does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information to browse pages.

Some pages offer tools and forms, such as The Cryptomath Score. When you use these tools, the site may process the inputs you submit to generate results.

The Cryptomath Score

The Cryptomath Score is a tool that helps analyze market setups based on the parameters you provide (for example an asset and a direction). Inputs are processed to compute results and display them back to you.

We do not ask for your name. We do not require an account to use the tool.

Cloudflare may process basic technical data (such as IP address and user agent) to deliver and protect the service.

Data retention

The tool is designed not to store the content of your Score inputs on the website. Standard server logs may exist for security and reliability purposes.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to the newsletter, your email address is collected and processed by Substack. Your subscription is managed by Substack and subject to their privacy policy and terms.

Analytics

This site does not run third party analytics scripts by default. Cloudflare may process basic request data (such as IP address and user agent) to deliver and protect the site.

Cookies

This site does not set advertising or tracking cookies. Third party services you use from links on this site may set their own cookies.

External links

This site contains links to external websites. When you click an external link, you are subject to the privacy policy of that third party.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach out via the Connect page.
