What this site does

thecryptomath.com is a mostly static website. It does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information to browse pages.

The Cryptomath Score

Some pages provide tools and forms, such as the Cryptomath Score at /score/. When you use these tools, the service may process the inputs you submit to generate results.

We do not ask for your name. You do not need an account to use the tool.

Cloudflare may process basic technical data (such as IP address and user agent) to deliver and protect the service.

Data retention

The tool is designed not to store the content of your Score inputs on the website. Standard server logs may exist for security and reliability purposes.

Newsletter

Analytics

Cookies

External links

This site contains links to external websites. When you click an external link, you are subject to the privacy policy of that third party.
