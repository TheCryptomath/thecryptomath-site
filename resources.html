Resources FAQ

Short answers about how these tools are selected and how to use this page.

How are these resources selected?

They come from repeated real use across daily research, wallet management, trading, DeFi tracking, and payments.

Are these the only tools worth using?

No. They are the current default choices in my stack, selected for clarity, reliability, and repeat usage rather than hype.

Is this page updated often?

It changes when my real stack changes, not whenever a new tool launches.

Where can I get more context on these picks?

The newsletter and public channels are where I explain why a tool stays in the stack, what I am testing, and what I no longer use.

Want more curated picks?

I share what stays in the stack, what leaves it, and what I am testing next.
