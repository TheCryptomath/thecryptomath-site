About this data

The Score Tool measures confluence across Macro, Technical and Psychology inputs. The score is experimental and has not demonstrated a reliable historical probability of success. Legacy Signal and Watchlist labels are therefore merged publicly as Experimental detection.

A detection is marked Active only during its first 24 hours. After that it remains visible as Research tracking until the backend tracking window ends. The +7d milestone is kept for transparency and research, not as a recommended holding period.

Prices are checked once per hour. Milestone columns capture the first observation after the target horizon, not the exact price at the precise minute. MFE and MAE are the best and worst directional moves observed during hourly checks, not tick-by-tick extremes.

The scanner does not provide a validated entry, stop, take-profit or expected return. What you see is what happened to price after each experimental detection. Nothing more, nothing less.

Experimental research only. Not a validated trading signal or investment advice.
