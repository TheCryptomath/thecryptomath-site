About this data

The Score Tool detects confluence on three pillars (Macro, Tech, Psycho) and emits four categories: Exceptional, Signal, Watch emergency, and Watchlist. Once emitted, each detection is tracked at +1h, +4h, +24h, +7d and +30d. Direction-correct means the price moved in the predicted direction at the time of the last update.

Prices are checked once per hour. Milestone columns (+24h, +7d) capture the first observation after that horizon, not the exact price at the precise minute. Observed MFE and MAE are the best and worst directional moves seen across these hourly checks, not tick-by-tick maxima.

No theoretical PnL is computed. The scanner does not build entries, stops or take-profits. What you see here is what happened to the price after each detection. Nothing more, nothing less.
